Frequently asked questions

What a logistics buyer usually wants to know before asking for a price.

What information should I send for a quote?

The route, loading date, freight type, weight, pallet count or volume, plus any loading or delivery requirements.

Can you handle both FTL and LTL?

Yes. The site positions the company around full truckload, partial loads and express transport depending on the job.

Do you only transport within Romania?

No. FANSOR SPEDITION is positioned for both domestic transport and international road freight within the European Union.

How quickly can you reply?

The operational goal is a fast initial response, usually on the same business day for complete and straightforward requests.

Do you offer ADR, refrigerated or oversized transport?

Those services are not presented as default confirmed offerings. Any special requirement should be discussed separately before quoting.

Why work with a smaller company?

For many businesses, the advantage is direct contact, less internal noise and a faster, more practical response to real shipping needs.
