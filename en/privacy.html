1. Data controller

This website is operated by FANSOR SPEDITION, a road freight company based in Suceava, Romania. The full legal company details can be added here once the final publication format is approved.

2. What data we may collect

Through the quote or contact forms we may collect:

  • company name and contact person name;
  • email address and phone number;
  • route, freight and requested transport date information;
  • any other details voluntarily entered in the notes fields.

3. Why we use the data

The information is used strictly for:

  • reviewing and replying to quote requests;
  • commercial and operational clarification regarding the requested shipment;
  • communication needed before a business collaboration starts;
  • better organisation of incoming transport requests.

4. Storage and sharing

Data is kept only for as long as needed to process the request and comply with relevant commercial or legal obligations. It is not sold and is not shared with third parties without a legitimate reason.

If the form is connected later to an external service such as Netlify Forms, Formspree or a custom backend, this section should be updated with the name of the chosen processor and its role.

5. Your rights

Under GDPR, you may request access to your data, correction, deletion or restriction of processing.

You may also ask how your data has been used and withdraw consent where consent is the relevant legal basis.
