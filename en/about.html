FANSOR SPEDITION started in a market where seriousness still matters.

Why being smaller can help

Not every customer needs a giant carrier.

  • Decisions can be made more directly and more quickly.
  • The conversation stays closer to the actual shipment and less tied to internal bureaucracy.
  • There is more flexibility for loads that do not fit a rigid standard pattern.
  • The relationship can become more personal and easier to build over time.
