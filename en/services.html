Choosing the right service

FTL, LTL or express?

  • Choose FTL when the load fills most of the useful capacity or needs a dedicated route.
  • Choose LTL when the shipment is smaller and cost efficiency matters.
  • Choose express when response time matters more than a standard transport pattern.
  • For recurring flows, share the estimated frequency and loading windows from the start.
